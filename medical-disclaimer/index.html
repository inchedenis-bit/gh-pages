Consult Your Healthcare Provider

You are strongly advised to consult with a qualified healthcare provider before beginning any fasting, dietary, or mineral supplementation protocol, including the Terra sequence.

This is especially important if you:

  • Have been diagnosed with diabetes (type 1 or type 2)
  • Take diabetes medication (insulin, metformin, sulfonylureas, or others)
  • Take blood pressure medication
  • Take blood thinners
  • Have kidney disease
  • Have gout
  • Have a thyroid condition
  • Are pregnant or nursing
  • Have a BMI below 18.5 (underweight)
  • Have a history of eating disorders
  • Are taking any prescription medication
  • Have any chronic health condition

Your healthcare provider is the only person qualified to advise you on whether fasting, dietary changes, or mineral supplementation is appropriate for your specific situation.

No Guarantee of Results

Terra does not guarantee that the sequence will produce any specific outcome. Individual results vary significantly.

Some participants have achieved reversal of their type 2 diabetes. Others have achieved significant improvement in their condition. Others may see limited or no improvement. Past participant outcomes do not guarantee future results for any individual.

Terra does not claim that the sequence cures, treats, or prevents any disease. The term "reversal" is used to describe the normalization of blood glucose without medication. It is not a claim of permanent cure.

Medication Disclaimer

Do not stop, start, or adjust any medication based on information provided through the Service. Any changes to your medication regimen must be made under the supervision of your prescribing physician.

Fasting can affect blood glucose, blood pressure, and electrolyte levels. These effects may alter your medication requirements. Your physician is the only person qualified to determine whether and how your medications should be adjusted.

Terra is not responsible for any adverse effects resulting from medication changes made without medical supervision.

Fasting, dietary changes, and mineral supplementation carry inherent risks. These risks include but are not limited to:

  • Hypoglycemia (dangerously low blood glucose)

  • Headaches and fatigue
  • Interactions with medications

By using the Service or participating in the Terra program, you voluntarily assume all risks associated with fasting, dietary changes, and mineral supplementation.
