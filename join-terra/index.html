Join Terra

Terra is a small, volunteer-run collective dedicated to terrain restoration. We teach a three-phase sequence of intermittent fasting, full-spectrum mineral salts, and broth from indigenous cattle. Our work is rooted in traditional African pastoral knowledge and supported by emerging research on autophagy, mineral metabolism, and gut health.

We have is a clear mission, a growing community of participants, and a commitment to honest, evidence-informed education.

We are looking for a few people to help with research and writing.

Why Terra Exists

Chronic diseases such as type 2 diabetes, hypertension, and metabolic syndrome have reached epidemic levels across Africa and the world. The conventional approach focuses on managing symptoms with lifelong medication. Terra offers a different path: restoring the internal terrain so the body can regulate itself.

Our participants have documented meaningful improvements. Some have reversed conditions they were told were irreversible. We need to document these outcomes, organize the research that supports our approach, and make this knowledge accessible to more people.

We cannot do this alone. We need your help.

Who We Are Looking For

You do not need years of experience. You need curiosity, attention to detail, and a few hours per week.

Ideal candidates include:

  • University students in health sciences, nutrition, biology, journalism, or communications
  • Early-career writers or researchers seeking experience and portfolio pieces
  • Individuals interested in traditional food systems, metabolic health, or medical anthropology

Terra values clarity, honesty, and independence. We do not promise what we cannot deliver. We expect the same from you.

What You Gain

Terra is transparent about compensation. Our stipends are modest. We are a small collective. We pay what we can.

RoleMonthly StipendAdditional Benefits
Case Study WriterKSh 7,000 - 9,000Published writing samples, reference letter, interviewing experience

Both roles are remote. Stipends are paid via M-Pesa at the end of each month based on completed and approved work.

What We Ask of You

  • Consistent communication (respond to messages within 2-3 days)
  • Meeting agreed deadlines or communicating early if you cannot
  • Respect for participant confidentiality and consent
  • Honest reporting of time and challenges

Terra is volunteer-led. Delays affect real people. If you need flexibility, that is fine. Just communicate.

The Work Environment

All communication happens via email and call. You will work independently. You will need your own computer and a reliable internet access.

We do not micromanage. We provide templates, guidelines, and regular check-ins. You manage your own schedule.

Our Commitment to You

Terra is volunteer-run. Our stipends are modest. We cannot compete with corporate salaries. What we offer is real work that helps real people. Your case studies will be published. Your research summaries will be used. You will have a direct impact on Terra's ability to reach more participants and document more outcomes. If you want to build skills, gain experience, and contribute to a mission-driven collective, you have found the right place.

Terra is an educational framework. It is not a medical treatment, diagnosis, or cure. Volunteer roles are remote, unpaid except for stated stipends, and open to applicants from East Africa.
