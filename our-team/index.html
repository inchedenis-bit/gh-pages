Our Team

Structure

Terra is a small, volunteer-run collective. We are not a corporation or a registered organization. We are a group of individuals who reversed our own chronic conditions using the Terra sequence and chose to make the salts and methodology available to others.

No one on this team draws a salary. All contributions are voluntary. This structure allows us to keep the program affordable and focused on restoration rather than revenue.

Core Members

Founder

The founder reversed his own type 2 diabetes twelve years ago using frequent short intermittent fasts, mineral salts, and broth from indigenous cattle. He spent years living among and learning from pastoral communities in Turkana, Maasailand, and other regions. His background is in research, not medicine. He does not hold himself out as a physician or a guru. He is a person who found a method that worked and chose to share it.

Research Coordinator

The research coordinator manages documentation of traditional salt sources, mineral compositions, and participant outcomes. She has traveled to the Baleni spring in South Africa, the Omo River in Ethiopia, and other traditional harvesting sites to verify sourcing and document methods. Her work ensures that the salts provided through the program are authentic and ethically sourced.

Community Moderator

The community moderator manages the private member forum and WhatsApp group. She ensures that discussions remain respectful, that questions receive answers, and that members support one another. She has completed the full three-month rotation program and maintains the practice.

Advisors

Terra consults with several individuals who provide guidance on safety, protocol adjustments, and participant screening. These advisors have backgrounds in nutrition, fasting research, and traditional food systems. They are not employees. They contribute their time because they believe in the work.

Volunteers

From time to time, former program participants volunteer to assist with onboarding new members, answering common questions, and sharing their experiences. These volunteers are not trained as medical professionals. They share what worked for them personally. Their contributions are acknowledged but not compensated.

What We Are Not

Terra is not a medical practice. No member of the team is licensed to diagnose, treat, or cure any disease. We do not provide medical advice. We provide an educational framework and access to rare, traditionally harvested salts.

We are not a registered non-profit organization. We are a collective of individuals who pool resources to source salts and maintain the program. There is no board of directors, no headquarters, and no paid staff.

We are not affiliated with any religious or spiritual movement. The Terra sequence is a set of dietary practices. No belief system is required to participate.

Why This Structure Exists

The decision to remain a volunteer-run collective was deliberate. Paid programs often prioritize revenue over outcomes. Corporate structures introduce conflicts of interest. By keeping Terra small and voluntary, we ensure that the only incentive is restoration.

We do not seek to scale. We do not seek to franchise. We seek to serve a limited number of participants who are ready for deep terrain repair.

Communication Expectations

Because the team is composed of volunteers, response times may be slower than commercial services. We typically respond to inquiries within three days. During busy periods, responses may take longer. We appreciate patience and understanding.

We do not operate a call center. We do not use chatbots. When you receive a response, it comes from a real person.

How to Reach Us

Inquiries about the program should be directed through the contact form on this website. Eligibility reviews are conducted by the founder and research coordinator. Community support questions are handled by the community moderator.

We do not publish individual email addresses or phone numbers to protect the privacy of our volunteers.
